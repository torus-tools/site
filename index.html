Even though they all offer the necessary infrastructure/services to host a website, the process to achieve this is usally not as simple as it seems... But don't worry, We are changing this.

How it Works

Torus Hosting is made up of a CLI and 3 SDK's; Stack, Domains, and Content. The CLI facilitates interaction with the SDK's through the comfrot of your command line but you can also use the SDK's programmatically inside your javascript projects. The SDK's manily standardize interaction with various services from different cloud providers; including both major cloud providers as well as domain registrars. Right now we only feature support for AWS and godaddy as they are respectively the cloud provider and domain registrar with the largest market shares, but we will continue exapnding this list.
